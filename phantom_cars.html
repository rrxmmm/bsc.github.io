Vehicle Description

- Rolls Royce Phantom from 2004, Color combination: Sapphire blue with beige leather interior, Only 50.700 km from new and in great condition, Desirable 6.8L V12 Engine producing up to 460 HP. The car was always maintained with the highest care and was never damaged. The 2003 Rolls-Royce Phantom was the first Rolls-Royce to be developed and launched under the wings of BMW. The 2003 Phantom was powered by a 6750 cc V12 rated at 460 hp. The engine was mated to a six-speed automatic transmission. The car also received a number of new features. For example, there is the power reserve gauge that indicates how much engine power is available. The rear doors are hinged to the rear and are secured by an electronic lock. Those rear doors also contain an umbrella. The distinctive Spirit of Ecstasy figure atop the equally distinctive radiator grille is electrically retractable. Finally, the RR logo on the wheel covers can rotate independently so that it remains upright at all times.
